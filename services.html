Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How long does the vehicle sourcing process take?
Typically 2-4 weeks for standard vehicles, 4-8 weeks for rare or luxury models. We provide regular updates throughout the process.
What countries do you ship to?
We ship worldwide to over 50 countries including Nigeria, Ghana, UAE, USA, UK, Canada, and most European countries.
Do you handle customs and import duties?
Yes, we handle all customs clearance, import duties, and documentation. We provide a complete breakdown of all costs upfront.
What is your vehicle inspection process?
Each vehicle undergoes a 150-point inspection covering mechanical, electrical, cosmetic, and performance aspects. We provide a detailed inspection report.
Can I test drive a vehicle before purchase?
For local purchases, yes. For international purchases, we provide comprehensive video walkarounds and independent inspection reports.
